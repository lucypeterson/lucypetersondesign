Dangerous Ideas Book

"Dangerous Ideas on Design Education" is a manifesto written by James Victore. The book design incorporates digital photography of abandoned buildings to convey failure, which Victore is in favor of. The doodles in the design represent the innate self; you doodle without thinking, drawing what your mind does on its own. These two ideas from the essay are the ones that resonated most with me, which is what I pulled out to use for the design concepts.
