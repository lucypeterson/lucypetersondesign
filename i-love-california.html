I Love California Video

When creating a video that is driven by typography, the song "I Love California" by Be Calm Honcho came to mind because of the strong imagery provoked by the lyrics. I created this project in collaboration with Terry Chen, a fellow graphic designer.

The song evokes a nostalgic feeling for the California scenery that I grew up in, which is what inspired the video. Some of the reference imagery I was looking at early on was vintage post cards, this gave me the idea to mask out the typography as the scenery passes through it. Using environmental typography was something I hadn't done in such a physical way before, but was a rewarding process to work on location and in real space.
