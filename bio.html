About Me
Lucy is a designer, curator, and day dreamer based in San Francisco. She is a recent graduate of California College of the Arts earning her BFA in graphic design with a minor in visual studies.

She started her design career not designing at all, but in tiny ballet slippers in a dance studio. Dance was her first creative outlet, and a lifelong passion. Later, she decided to become a designer because she saw it as a way to learn about all sorts of things through a creative lens.

With a particular interest in adding physicality to her graphic design practice, Lucy produces and curates art shows, thinking about experience, systems and environments. In her work she loves the nitty gritty of typography, designing systems, and creating environments.

Outside of her design work catch Lucy upside down in dance class on Saturday mornings or bird watching in Dolores Park with a bottle of kombucha.
