Design Division Thesis Show

In collaboration with Gráinne Hebeler and Taylor Bird we produced the Fall 2017 Design Division Thesis show at California College of the Arts. For the first time at CCA multiple design divisions came together to exhibit their thesis work in the same space.

Role: managing the printing and installation of the exhibition graphics, helping to build the installations in space (see the cardboard wall in the images). I also assisted the artists from all three disciplines install their work and de-installed the show, coordinating the donations of extra materials.

Learnings: This project allowed me to learn stronger project management skills, how to produce a successful event, and think about space and how tactility and dimensionality could exist in the work I do.
